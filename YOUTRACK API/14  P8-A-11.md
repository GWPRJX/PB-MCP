# Product Information
APIs for retrieving product details, categories, stock, and images.

✔ Mandatory
-- Optional

| Action                                  | URL                                       | Method | Parameters                                                                                                                                          |
| --------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- | ------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Retrieve product information**        | `/productmaster/{productId}`              | GET    | **✔ productId**,   `excludeInTransit` -- (default = false)                                                                                                                                 |
| **Retrieve storage info of a product**  | `/storage/{productId}/{warehouseId}`      | GET    | **✔ productId**, **✔ warehouseId**                                                                                                                  |
| **Retrieve Product Category List**      | `/productcategorylist`                    | GET    | `updatedSince` -- , `parentOnly` –, `offset` –, `limit` –  , `leafCategoryOnly` --                                                                        |
| **Retrieve categories under a parent**  | `/productcategorylist/{parentCategoryId}`       | GET    | **✔ parentCategoryId**, `offset` –, `limit` –                                                                                                                                      |
| **Retrieve Product List of a Category** | `/productlist/{categoryId}`               | GET    | **✔ categoryId**, `offset` –, `limit` –, `activeOnly` -- (default = true)                                                                                                                                     |
| **Retrieve Product List (since time)**  | `/productmaster/productlist`              | GET    | `updatedSince` ✔, `includeStockUpdate` – (default=true), `pricelistIds` --, `webstoreFeaturedOnly` –, `limitByOrg` –, `isactive` –, `skuType` –, `limit` –, `offset` –, `excludeInTransit` --,  `sortByName` -- (default = true), `baseProductId` -- |
| **Retrieve Product Image**              | `/productmaster/productimage/{productId}` | GET    | **✔ productId**, `imageType` – (POI / CTI / WBI)                                                                                                    |
| **Retrieve Product Image List**         | `/productmaster/productimagelist`         | GET    | `updatedSince` ✔, `imageType` – (POI / CTI / WBI), `offset` –, `limit` –                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
|**Retrieve storage info for products in all warehouses** | `/storage/list` | GET | `updatedSince` ✔, `productId` --, `webstoreFeaturedOnly` --, `offset` –, `limit` –, `excludeInTransit` --, `fetchASI` -- |
